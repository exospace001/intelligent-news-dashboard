## Reading Articles

### Article List
Each article displays:
- **Headline** - Main article title
- **Source** - Which website/blog it came from
- **Author** - Writer's name (when available)
- **Date** - When the article was published
- **Reading time** - Estimated minutes to read
- **Summary** - 2-3 sentence preview of the content

### Reading Articles
- **"Read article"** - Opens the full article on the original website
- Articles open in a new browser tab
- Your dashboard remains open for easy navigation

### Saving Articles
- Click **"Save"** to bookmark articles for later
- Click **"Unsave"** to remove from your saved list
- Use the **"Saved"** filter to see all bookmarked articles

## Organizing Your Reading

## Customizing Your Experience

### Theme Toggle
- Click **"Dark"** to switch to dark mode for evening reading
- Click **"Light"** to return to light mode
- Your preference is saved automatically

### Adding New Feeds
You can add RSS feeds in two ways:

#### Method 1: Web Interface
1. Click **"Add Feed"** in the filter bar
2. Enter the **RSS feed URL** (e.g., `https://example.com/feed`)
3. Enter a **name** for the feed
4. Select a **category** (WordPress, Design, Development, Other)
5. Click **"Add Feed"**
6. New articles will appear in your next refresh

#### Method 2: Tell Exo (Voice/Chat)
Simply say or type commands like:
- *"Add RSS feed: https://techcrunch.com/feed"*
- *"Subscribe to CSS-Tricks feed"*
- *"Add The Verge's RSS feed"*

Exo will automatically add the feed and categorize it appropriately.

### Refreshing Content
- Click **"Refresh"** to manually check for new articles
- The dashboard automatically fetches new content every 4 hours
- You'll see fresh articles appear at the top of your feed

## Troubleshooting

### Login Issues
If you can't log in:
- Double-check your username: `tam`
- Double-check your password: `news2024`
- Try refreshing the page
- Clear your browser's stored credentials

### No Articles Showing
If you don't see articles:
- Click **"Refresh"** to fetch latest content
- Try the **"All"** filter to see everything
- Check if you have an internet connection
- The initial load may take a few moments

### Slow Loading
If the dashboard is slow:
- Check your internet connection speed
- Try refreshing the page
- Clear your browser cache
- Close other browser tabs using bandwidth

### Add Feed Not Working
If you can't add a new feed:
- Verify the RSS feed URL is correct and accessible
- Make sure the URL starts with `http://` or `https://`
- Some websites don't provide RSS feeds
- Try finding the feed URL in the website's footer or "RSS" link

### Articles Not Updating
If you're not seeing new content:
- Click **"Refresh"** manually
- Check that the original websites are publishing new content
- Some feeds update infrequently
- The automatic refresh happens every 4 hours

## Getting Help

### Common Questions

**Q: How often does content update?**
A: Automatically every 4 hours, or manually with the "Refresh" button.

**Q: Can I read articles offline?**
A: The article list and summaries work offline, but full articles require internet connection.

**Q: How many articles are stored?**
A: The system keeps several hundred recent articles and automatically manages storage.

**Q: Can I export my saved articles?**
A: Currently no export feature, but saved articles remain available in your "Saved" filter.

**Q: What if a feed stops working?**
A: The system will continue trying to fetch from all feeds. Broken feeds won't affect other sources.
